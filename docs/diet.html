Essential Nutrients & Where to Get Them

  • Protein: Lentils, chickpeas, tofu, soy chunks, peanuts, quinoa
  • Calcium: Ragi, sesame seeds, amaranth, drumstick leaves
  • Iron: Dates, jaggery, rajma, spinach, black sesame seeds
  • Omega-3: Flaxseeds, chia seeds, walnuts
  • Vitamin B12: Needs supplementation
  • Vitamin D: Sunlight exposure, fortified plant milk, D3 supplement (vegan)
  • Zinc: Pumpkin seeds, sesame seeds, cashews
  • Magnesium: Whole grains, almonds, leafy greens
  • Iodine: Iodized salt, seaweed (in moderation)
